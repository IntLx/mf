Sharjah Ruler Inaugurates Wheat Farm 1st Phase in Mleiha

May 20, 2023

The ruler of Sharjah, His Highness Sheikh Sultan bin Muhammad Al Qasimi, inaugurated the first phase of the wheat farm in Mleiha today. The project aims to enhance the agricultural sector and promote self-sufficiency in wheat production.

The wheat farm spans over an area of 500 acres and utilizes advanced agricultural techniques to ensure high-quality and sustainable production. The farm is equipped with state-of-the-art irrigation systems and modern machinery to optimize the cultivation process.

In his speech during the inauguration ceremony, His Highness emphasized the importance of investing in agriculture and supporting local farmers. He highlighted the role of such initiatives in achieving food security and contributing to the country's overall development.

The inauguration ceremony was attended by government officials, local farmers, and representatives from the agricultural sector. They expressed their appreciation for the ruler's continuous efforts in advancing the agricultural industry and providing opportunities for sustainable growth.

This milestone marks a significant step forward in Sharjah's agricultural sector, and it is expected to contribute to the region's economic diversification and food security goals. The wheat farm's future phases will further expand its operations and increase wheat production capacity in Mleiha.
